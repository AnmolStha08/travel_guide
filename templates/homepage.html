Illam

Illam, a picturesque district nestled in the eastern hills of Nepal, captivates visitors with its lush tea gardens, rolling green hills, and serene ambiance. Renowned as the "Tea Garden of Nepal," Illam boasts sprawling plantations that carpet the landscape, offering a mesmerizing sight and a source of livelihood for local communities. The region's temperate climate and fertile soil create optimal conditions for tea cultivation, resulting in some of Nepal's finest tea varieties
